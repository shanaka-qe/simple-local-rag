## Returns & Refunds

- Refund window: **30 days** from the delivery date.
- Items must be returned in their original packaging.
- Refunds are processed within **5 business days** of receiving the return.
- Restocking fee: none for unopened items; **15%** for opened items.

## Warranty

- Standard warranty: **24 months** from the date of purchase.
- Covers manufacturing defects only — not accidental damage or water damage.
- Warranty claims require the order number and proof of purchase.

## RMA (Return Merchandise Authorization) Process

1. Email support with your order number (format: `NB-XXXXXX`).
2. Receive an RMA code within **1 business day**.
3. Ship the item with the RMA code clearly visible on the package.
4. Track the return status via the online support portal.
