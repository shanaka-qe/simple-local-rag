## Mission Objective

Map and sample hydrothermal vent ecosystems in the Mariana back-arc basin at
depths exceeding 4,000 metres.

## Key Findings

- Discovered **3** previously undocumented species of tubeworm.
- Recorded a maximum dive depth of **4,812 metres** using the submersible *Abyssal-1*.
- Collected **214** biological samples and **1.2 TB** of sonar mapping data.
